Relax Unwind Enjoy

Your private getaway in southern Spain.

Condado de Alhama is a picturesque resort located in the heart of Murcia, Spain, surrounded by stunning natural landscapes. This vibrant community offers a range of modern amenities, including a golf course, swimming pools, and various dining options. Visitors can enjoy a variety of recreational activities, from hiking and cycling in the nearby mountains to exploring the local markets. The resort's welcoming atmosphere makes it an ideal destination for families and individuals seeking a relaxing getaway.

Apartment Features:

  • Sleeps six
  • Two double and one single bedrooms
  • Fully furnisted and equiped
  • Air conditioning throughout
  • Towels and bedding provided
  • Two outdoor terraces with awning
  • Washing machine
  • Less than 50m from pool
  • Spanish tourist board certified accommodation

Location

The apartment is located 43km (26 miles) from Murcia and 16 miles from the port town of Mazzaron, known for it's beaches and clear water. It is only a short drive to the historic town of Cartagena and the holiday resorts on the Mar Menor.
