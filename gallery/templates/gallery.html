The apartment

Discover a perfect retreat for up to six guests in this fully furnished and equipped apartment. Featuring two double bedrooms and a single bedroom, you'll enjoy air conditioning throughout and provided towels and bedding for your comfort. Relax on one of the two outdoor terraces with an awning, or take advantage of the fully equipped kitchen. Located less than 50 meters from the pool, this Spanish Tourist Board certified accommodation ensures a delightful stay.

Condado De Alhama Resort

Condado de Alhama Resort is a luxurious retreat offering a wide array of amenities and activities for guests. The resort features beautifully landscaped gardens, multiple swimming pools, and sports facilities, including tennis courts and a golf course designed by Jack Nicklaus. Guests can enjoy a variety of dining options with several on-site restaurants and bars, as well as a well-equipped fitness center and spa.
